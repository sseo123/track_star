Why Run to a Beat?

Running with music at a specific BPM helps your body naturally sync your steps to the beat, making your pace more consistent and efficient.

How to run at the tempo:

Pick a song with your target BPM, then match each step (or every other step) to the beat—left foot, right foot, in time with the music.
