Why Run to a Beat?

Because who doesn't love running to music. But seriously, running with music at a specific BPM helps your body naturally sync your steps to the beat, allowing your pace to be more consistent.

How to run at the tempo:

Pick a song with or near your target BPM, then match each step to the beat as you run! I've carefully selected my favorite running songs, amazing right?
